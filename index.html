02 — about

Hey! I'm Neha — a software engineer who lives at the intersection of mobile apps, clean code and a good cup of masala chai.

What started as curiosity turned into a habit of building, breaking, and refining until things just feel right. I’ve always been drawn to what happens behind the scenes — which led me to crafting mobile apps that aren’t just functional, but thoughtfully designed.

Now I team up with founders and small teams to take React Native apps from idea to the app stores — smooth, reliable, and built to last. I care about performance, clean architecture, and the small details most people overlook (the ones that quietly make an app feel good). When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me exploring new ideas, sharpening my skills, or enjoying that perfect cup of chai that fuels it all.
